# 🌸 Divine Feminine – AI Powered Women’s Health Companion

Divine Feminine is an **AI-powered digital health companion built to empower women with knowledge, tools, and support to better understand and manage their health**.

The platform combines **cycle tracking, mental wellness tools, AI assistance, health diagnostics, and access to medical professionals** in one unified dashboard designed specifically for women.

Our mission is simple:

> **Help women understand their bodies, predict health patterns, and access care faster.**

---

# 🚩 Problem Statement

Women’s health is often **fragmented across multiple apps and resources**.

Most tools only provide **basic period tracking**, leaving out critical aspects of women’s well-being such as:

- Mental health monitoring  
- Hormonal condition awareness (PCOS, anemia)  
- Nutrition guidance  
- Exercise recommendations  
- Access to healthcare professionals  
- Secure storage of medical data  

Because of this fragmentation, women struggle to get **holistic insights into their health**.

Divine Feminine solves this problem by bringing **all essential women’s health tools into a single intelligent platform**.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

Divine Feminine is a **centralized women's health ecosystem** that helps users:

- Track their menstrual cycle
- Monitor emotional and physical well-being
- Receive AI-powered insights
- Log symptoms and mood patterns
- Connect with medical specialists
- Store medical records securely

By combining **data tracking, AI assistance, and medical accessibility**, the platform becomes a **complete digital health companion**.

---

# ✨ Key Features

## 🌸 Period & Cycle Tracker
The cycle tracker helps users understand their reproductive health through:

- Period logging
- Cycle length prediction
- Fertility window estimation
- Phase detection (Menstrual, Follicular, Ovulation, Luteal)
- Visual cycle dashboard

This allows users to **predict their cycle and understand hormonal changes**.

---

## 🧠 Mental Health & Mood Journal
Mental well-being is a critical part of overall health.

Divine Feminine includes:

- Mood tracking
- Stress level logging
- Personal journaling
- Emotional insights

Users can reflect on their emotional patterns and understand how **hormones influence mood and energy levels**.

---

## 🤖 AI Wellness Assistant
The platform includes an **AI-powered assistant** that helps users with:

- Mental wellness support
- Stress and anxiety guidance
- Health-related questions
- Personalized self-care suggestions

The assistant acts as a **24/7 health companion**.

---

## 🥗 Nutrition Planner
Nutrition recommendations tailored to women’s health:

- Iron-rich diet suggestions
- Hydration reminders
- Nutritional guidance for hormonal balance
- Cycle-phase based nutrition insights

This helps users maintain **healthy hormone levels and improved energy**.

---

## 🏃 Exercise & Yoga Guidance
Physical activity recommendations based on cycle phases:

- Energy-based workout suggestions
- Yoga routines
- Gentle exercises during menstruation
- High-energy workouts during ovulation

This allows users to **work with their body instead of against it**.

---

## 🧬 PCOS Risk Checker
PCOS affects millions of women but is often diagnosed late.

This tool helps users:

- Assess symptoms
- Identify potential risk factors
- Receive early awareness insights

Early detection can encourage **timely medical consultation**.

---

## 💊 Anemia Scanner
Iron deficiency is one of the most common health issues among women.

This feature allows users to:

- Check common anemia symptoms
- Receive diet suggestions
- Track fatigue and energy patterns

Helping users **identify potential iron deficiency early**.

---

## 🩺 Find a Doctor
Divine Feminine connects users with **verified specialist doctors**, including:

- Gynecologists
- Endocrinologists
- Nutritionists
- Mental health professionals

This bridges the gap between **digital insights and real medical care**.

---

## 📂 Health Wallet
A secure digital vault where users can store:

- Medical reports
- Prescriptions
- Blood test results
- Health history

This keeps health data **organized and accessible whenever needed**.

---

## 🚨 Emergency SOS
An emergency support feature that allows users to quickly access **urgent assistance when needed**.

---

# 🧠 AI & Data Insights

Divine Feminine uses user input data to generate insights such as:

- Cycle phase predictions

- Stress pattern recognition
- Lifestyle recommendations

These insights help users make **better health decisions based on data**.
